The idea

I take something ordinary
and return it mythic.

Pets become cosmic beings. Moods become oceans. Memories become tracks you can play back. These tools aren't just fun — they make people feel seen. That's rarer and harder to copy than any feature.

Each tool is a small act of attention. A moment where technology stops being cold and becomes something close to tender.

Think of this as a cabinet of delights. Each new specimen teaches something new — about image generation, about emotion, about what it means to make something personal at scale.

Always asking the same question: does this make someone feel like the universe noticed them?

About Irina

Making things that
feel like love

I'm a product designer and artist who builds tools of delight — small apps that sit at the intersection of AI and emotion. My tools are personal, playful, and designed to make you feel something real. irina.love is the home for everything I'm building, one curiosity at a time.
